About This Computer

Author: Gavin Ruane

About Me: I'm a second-year student at Cal Poly in San Luis Obispo, California studying computer engineering, and this is my personal website! I like to update this site with projects I've created, typeset notes for classes I've taken, and my thoughts on various forms of media.

About Website: If you know me, you know I love retro computers—especially classic Macintoshes—which is why I've themed my site around the classic Mac OS. Recently, I've been working on "upgrading" my site from System 6 to Mac OS 9, which is what you're seeing today.

Thanks To: The "upgrade" to Mac OS 9 wouldn't be possible without the following projects from other cool Internet users:

  • Platinum by Robbie Bird, a CSS/JS UI framework replicating Mac OS components (modified for my site)
  • Virtue (font) by Marty P. Pfeiffer, a readable Charcoal-like font
