Final Praxis Project

TITLE OF THIS PROJECT

Welcome to my Final US Experience Praxis Project web page! My name is Gavin Ruane, a student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, and I have created and shared this website not only as an assignment for WGQS 201 but also a personal synthesis and collection of the knowledge I've accrued throughout this course.

One of the reasons why I'm publishing this project on my website—along with some other Praxis projects—is because I want to contribute to the vast expanse of thought and discussion on the Internet about topics like feminism, the meaning of gender, intersectionality, and more. No, I'm not an expert on these topics, but I care deeply about them, and I hope to join the conversations occurring out in the world with some of my contributions.

How to Navigate

You've started in the right place! Use the "Contents" sidebar to the right to navigate from page to page. I recommend that you go through this "mixtape" in the order I've arranged. To return back to this initial page at any time, click the "Start Here!" link in the sidebar, or click the white square in the corner of this window.

About This Project

This is a project created by Gavin Ruane, a student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo in Dr. Ednie Kaeh Garrison's WGQS 201 course. All analysis and written commentary on the materials included in this "mixtape" are my own work. The materials themselves—videos, music, audio, etc.—are the property of their creators and are cited on the Works Cited part of this project. I hope you enjoy this project!

Additional Information

If you want to download the accompanying Dialectical Journal for this project, scroll to the bottom of the sidebar to the left and select your desired file format. You can download a PDF of the assignment or an ODT (LibreOffice Writer) document that can be edited.
