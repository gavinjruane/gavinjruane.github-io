Dublin Coast

At the easternmost edge of the county (and the state of Oxnard) lies the Dublin Coast, a rural beachside region of San Clemente. Located around 50 miles from central Inland Heights and 70 miles from the Rocchio empire of San Clemente, the Dublin Coast exists as a rural alternative to the bustling city life of the San Clemente urban region.

Inland Heights

With roots as an experimental development community, Inland Heights today holds a legacy of being one of the most influential regions of Oxnard, Ashland, and the world. Characterized by its sprawling, car-oriented design, the Inland Heights region provides a taste of urban life in a quieter, suburban setting.

Inland Heights
Population 686,595
Type City
Current Mayor Juliette Lewis

The City of Inland Heights is the center of urban and cultural life in the Inland Heights region of San Clemente County. Central districts like Downtown, Arts District, Loma Linda, and Rubidoux Plaza emulate the dense urban feel of the Rocchio and Gateway Park empires in San Clemente, while surrounding districts and far-out neighborhoods take on a more suburban feel.

Inland Heights began development in 1901 when residents of San Clemente began searching for a less urban but still relevant alternative to fast-paced San Clemente city life. The historic Loomis Ranch neighborhood in west-central Inland Heights marks the birthplace of the city.

Rocchio Mountain

Rpcchio Mountian encompasses the central urban region of the city of San Clemente and San Clemente County. Ashland's Capitol Plaza, located in the Rocchio Mountain region, serves as the capitol of the nation. Additionally, the Central Business District of San Clemente, the University of Oxnard at San Clemente, the University of Ashland, and many other important landmarks are located within the Rocchio Mountain region.

  • San Clemente Rocchio
  • Rocchio Mountain
  • Spartan

South Inland Valley

In San Clemente County, the South Inland Valley is primarily known as a newer and more industrial region of the area. Many important industrial corporations as well as infrastructural institutions—including the Miriam Temple International Airport—exist in this region. Additionally, many lower-density housing neigborhoods and community districts are located in this area.

  • San Clemente South Inland Centre
  • Promenade
  • Montara
  • Williams
  • Rosenberg

Vista Coast

The Vista Coast region of San Clemente County largely represents the Gateway Park empire and its surrounding jurisdictions, as well as some of the cities in south Vista Real County (not listed here). This region is primarily characterized as a dense urban residential region with numerous cultural and community districts. Additionally, many educational and entertainment institutions in Ashland maintain locations and headquarters in the Vista Coast region.

  • San Clemente Gateway Park
  • West San Clemente
  • Ocean Gardens
  • Cherry Park
  • Sumter Point
  • Calipatria
