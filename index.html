About Me

Hi there, I'm Gavin Ruane! I'm a student based in San Francisco, California, and you've just stumbled upon my website!

On this website, I like to type up my calculus notes and share them with others. Using \(\LaTeX\) and MathJax, I typeset my math notes so that they're accessible and professional. I mean, who wants to download some nasty PDF when you can use the amazing technologies built into your browser and JavaScript?

I also like to share some of my projects that I work on in school, like my computer science projects or maybe some big English assignment. But I probably won't share these because this freaky girl from Calabasas found a YouTube video of mine explaining my project and demanded that I send her my work. Uh...no? Do your own work!

Have any questions? Feel free to email me! (Note: I don't always check my Outlook email right away, so don't expect an immediate response!)
