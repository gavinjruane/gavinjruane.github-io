 "It Was Mr. Glosser!"

Hi there! My name is Gavin! I'm a second-year student at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo, and this is my official website. I host various things on my website ranging from my thoughts on random phenomena and personal experiments to school projects and formal writings.

As you may have noticed, I have moved on from my website design emulating the Classic Macintosh. As much as it pains me to leave that beautiful design behind—especially considering the hours of work I put into toying with flexbox—I need a more modern approach for my site. As always, my website designs are open source and available on my GitHub, so please feel free to use my design—with attribution, of course!

The Living End

dir. Gregg Araki

This was such a cool watch. Being my first Gregg Araki film, I had no idea what to expect, and The Living End exceeded whatever expectations I had.

Not only was this beautifully filmed, but it was also a powerful look into the perspectives of HIV positive men in the late ´80s and early ´90s.

Gregg Araki rocks.
